What is Photography?

Photography is a hobby where you use a camera to capture moments and scenes. People enjoy it because it helps them save memories, be creative, and see things in a new way.

There are lots of types of photography. You can take portraits of people, shoot landscapes of nature, capture wildlife, or even do close-ups of tiny details.

Photography TypeDescription
PortraitCaptures people, their expressions, and personalities.
LandscapeFocuses on natural scenes like mountains, lakes, and sunsets.
MacroClose-up photography that captures small details and subjects.

When is the Best Time?

The golden hour is a favorite for many photographers due to its warm and soft lighting. Early morning and late afternoon provide optimal conditions.

Night photography can capture stunning long-exposure shots of stars, city lights, and more.

Time of DayBest For
Golden HourPortraits, landscapes
Blue HourCityscapes, moody shots
NightAstrophotography, long exposure
